Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

It's very good and I like to eat it all the time. My name is Garfield and I hate mondays.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Sausage
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until they are al dente.
  2. Layer Sauce, noodles, cheese, and meat in a baking dish
  3. Bake in oven
  4. Enjoy!
